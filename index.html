The neural network was trained on the dataset of about 500 images of Brooks River bears. This resulted in 4 cooperating YOLOv8 models:

  1. detecting a bear figure
  2. classifying a bear by the detected figure
  3. detecting a bear's head within the detected figure
  4. classifying a bear by the detected head

There's still much to do:

  1. imroove the dataset (possible only in seazon)
  2. train better models; avoid recognizing false positives, becouse it still can see a bear in a picture of a bread loaf
